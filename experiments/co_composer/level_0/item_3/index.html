Artist Beach House, Genre synthpop

The old persists alongside and despite the new. 
            I appoach paths as not only solitary places but also sociable ones,
            where once silenced voices might be heard.
            I feel the wind-stirred trees at an old crossroads give whispered voice,
            a now vanished village they eavesdrop on the clink, the roar, 
            the hum of old smithies and inns.
